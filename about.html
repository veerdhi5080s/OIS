Terna Orchids the International School

Orchids The International School is renowned for turning learning into a fun experience. In spite of just focusing on theory and grades, our teachers make sure that kids are happy and understand the foundational concepts. We integrate technology with the curriculum so our students can match the ever-evolving world. Our optimal digital classrooms are equipped to enhance the learning experience with digital boards, 3D animated videos, innovative tools, and unique assessments to make the classroom fun. Orchids design engaging academic experiences that include robotics, financial literacy, public speaking, astronomy, etc. Our Central Academic & Research Team (CART), composed of highly qualified and experienced academicians, provides curriculum, training, and support to all our schools across India to ensure standardization. Students discover their interests, talents, and the joy of learning through experiences inside the classroom, STEM labs, and across the campus.
