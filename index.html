Terna Orchids the International School

Located in the advanced region of Koparkhairane, ORCHIDS The International School is recognized as one of the top CBSE schools in the area. With a network of over 90 CBSE schools in more than 25 cities across India, ORCHIDS The International School prioritizes the overall development of students over grades through a holistic approach to education and hands-on learning. We aim to instill values of responsible global citizenship and critical thinking in our students from a young age, and our innovative learning tools, SHARPER, are founded on five core principles that foster a secure and supportive environment for our students to flourish in. As a leading CBSE school in Koparkhairane, we understand the importance of nurturing each student to reach their full potential in all aspects of life. We are committed to providing world-class facilities and personalized attention to help our students become confident and accomplished individuals, equipped with the skills necessary to thrive in the future.
